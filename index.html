Manufacturing Company

50 Employees

Challenge: Struggling with inaccurate financial reports and cash flow visibility. Multiple spreadsheets led to errors and delayed decision-making.

Solution: Implemented QuickBooks Online with custom job costing, integrated their inventory system, and created a real-time KPI dashboard showing cash flow, job profitability, and vendor spend.

Results:

  • Reduced month-end close time from 15 days to 3 days
  • Improved cash flow visibility, eliminating late vendor payments
  • Identified unprofitable jobs, leading to 18% margin improvement
  • Saved 20+ hours per week on manual data entry

Professional Services Firm

Growing Startup

Challenge: Rapid growth outpaced their financial systems. No clear visibility into project profitability or resource allocation. Founder spending 15+ hours weekly on bookkeeping.

Solution: Deployed our Fractional CFO services with QuickBooks ProAdvisor support. Created project-based tracking, automated invoicing, and built custom dashboards for client profitability and resource utilization.

Results:

  • Founder reclaimed 15 hours weekly to focus on client work
  • Discovered their most profitable client segment, doubled down on marketing
  • Increased average project margin from 32% to 47%
  • Successfully raised $500K in funding with clean, investor-ready financials

E-Commerce Retailer

Multi-Channel Sales

Challenge: Selling across Amazon, Shopify, and wholesale channels created reconciliation nightmares. Couldn't track true profitability per channel or SKU.

Solution: Integrated all sales channels into QuickBooks Online, implemented automated inventory tracking, and created a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard showing channel performance, SKU profitability, and inventory turnover.

Results:

  • Eliminated 40+ hours monthly spent on manual reconciliation
  • Identified and discontinued 15 unprofitable SKUs
  • Optimized inventory levels, reducing carrying costs by $45K annually
  • Increased overall profitability by 24% through data-driven decisions

Real Estate Investor

12-Property Portfolio

Challenge: Managing a diverse portfolio with 4 STRs, 3 MTRs, 3 LTRs, plus active wholesaling and joint venture partnerships. Creative financing structures and multiple entities made tracking profitability per property and strategy nearly impossible.

Solution: Implemented QuickBooks with class tracking for each property and strategy type. Set up entity-specific books with consolidated reporting. Created dashboards tracking cash-on-cash return, occupancy rates by rental type, wholesale deal flow, and partnership distributions.

Results:

  • Identified that MTRs outperformed STRs by 22% after all expenses
  • Discovered two LTRs were underperforming - refinanced and increased NOI by $18K annually
  • Automated partnership K-1 preparation, saving $8K in accounting fees
  • Real-time visibility enabled 3 additional wholesale deals through improved cash position awareness
  • Successfully secured $2.3M in financing with clean, organized financials by entity

Common Pain Points:

  • Job costing inaccuracies leading to unprofitable projects

  • Retainage management and cash flow gaps

  • Job-specific QuickBooks setup with accurate cost tracking
  • Real-time profitability dashboards by project and crew
  • Automated time tracking and payroll integration
  • Cash flow forecasting including payment schedules
